classify_sperm_whale_audio() — ICI-based coda classifier:
  Fine-grained click detection (hop=64, delta=0.005)
  ICI stats: mean, CV (coefficient of variation), slope per coda
  Tiers:
    two simultaneous trains                  → paradox
    CV ≥ 0.5 (highly irregular rubato)      → paradox
    CV 0.4-0.5, |slope| > 0.5ms/click       → diphthong (direction by slope sign)
    CV 0.4-0.5, |slope| ≤ 0.5              → paradox
    CV < 0.4, mean_ICI ≤ 65ms              → i-coda (up) or ī-coda (up evalt)
    CV < 0.4, mean_ICI ≥ 85ms              → a-coda (dn)
    CV < 0.4, 65-85ms                       → diphthong or link by slope
  All codas: + fix + anc

translate_wav(): species= parameter dispatches to species-specific classifier
